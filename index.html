Coudersport, PA - Friday through Sunday,
The third weekend in July!

The 3rd Annual Eliot Ness Fest in Coudersport, Pa. This one-of-a-kind festival celebrates the life and career of the famous lawman, who spent the final two years of his life in Coudersport. It is designed to both entertain and educate the public, while spotlighting the law enforcement community, reliving the Roaring 20s with captivating activities and attractions, and drawing visitors to Potter County to join in on the fun.
